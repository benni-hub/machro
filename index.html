About MACHRO

MACHRO (Maracha Community Health and Empowerment Organization) is a grassroots nonprofit dedicated to improving lives in West Nile, Uganda. Founded by local leaders, we address critical needs in healthcare, education, and economic development.

Our approach combines direct service with community empowerment, ensuring sustainable change that comes from within the communities we serve.
