Our Story

Discover the journey of MACHRO and our impact in West Nile communities

Founded in 2017, MACHRO (Maracha Community Health Rights Organization) is a grassroots nonprofit based in Maracha District, Uganda. We were established to support vulnerable groups through health services and sustainable economic empowerment programs.

Our organization addresses critical challenges faced by vulnerable children, orphans, adolescents, persons with disabilities, people living with HIV, and both women and men in our communities. We promote sustainable development through education, training, and advocacy, creating opportunities that enhance well-being and uphold human rights.

What began as a local initiative has grown into a comprehensive community development organization, impacting thousands of lives across the West Nile region.

Our Vision

A healthy and economically sustainable community in West Nile where all members have equal opportunities to thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To build bridges for accessible diversified health service delivery and poverty eradication for vulnerable groups at the grassroots level in Northern Uganda.
