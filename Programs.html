Our Transformative Programs

Comprehensive initiatives addressing health, economic, education and advocacy needs in West Nile communities

Our Program Areas

MACHRO implements targeted programs across four key areas to create sustainable change in West Nile, Uganda. Each program is designed with community participation to ensure relevance and maximum impact.
